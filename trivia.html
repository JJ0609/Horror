Horror Movie Trivia

This trivia will have a mix of difficulty catering both to horror novices and hardcore gore hounds alike! This trivia will also cover all manners of Horror films, but will primarily focus on movies included on this list, but don't be surprised if you find yourself asked who the killer was in Friday the 13th.

Question

The Batman villian, the Joker, is based on Conrad Veidt's character in what 1928 film?

Answer

The Man Who Laughs

Question

Which famous magician appears in the 1980 film Terror Train?

Answer

David Copperfield

Question

In the 1932 film Freaks what is the fate of the acrobat Cleopatra?

Answer

She is turned into a human-duck Hybrid and put on display as a part of the "Freakshow"

Question

Boris Karloff had his breakout role in1931 with Frankenstein, this was his ___ film performance.

Answer

82nd
